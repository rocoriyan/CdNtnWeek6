Digital Card Games

Keelhaulin'

Keelhaulin' is a digital card game playable in Sims 2 DS and Sims 2 GBA. Two players will be randomly given 10 cards and the aim of each round is to be rid of them all. Once a player reaches 200 coins, they win and the game ends.
